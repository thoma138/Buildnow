BUILDNOW
  • About
    • BUILDNOW is a calculator that
      evaluates a rough estimate
      of the cost of new home
      builds in Australia 2022,
      depending on the quality option
      chosen and total square
      metreage of the build.
      There are four options to choose
      from, Budget ($1300 per m²),
      Value ($1900m²), Quality ($2400m²)
      and Luxury ($3900m²).
      Also displays the current
      Australian Property Price Index
